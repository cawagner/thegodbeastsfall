Credits

Most media is from OpenGameArt.org. Music is from the aptly-named Generic 8-bit JRPG Soundtrack by avgvst. Character sprites and portraits are by Antifarea, tiles are by surt and bart. The in-game dialogue font is Deathwake by the Pickford Brothers. Enemy sprites are mostly mine, but if something looks oddly well drawn, it might be by Antifarea instead during this prototype phase.

This game is obviously a WIP, but when it's either finished or abandoned, all of my original art will also be going back into the OGA. Thank you for a great resource! Likewise, the game's source will be MIT licensed - just not yet because some of the code still makes me cry.
